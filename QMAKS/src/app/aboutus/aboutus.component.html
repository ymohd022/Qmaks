Our History

QMAKS was established in 2010 with a vision to transform the construction industry through innovation and excellence. What began as a small team of dedicated professionals has grown into a leading construction company with a portfolio of successful projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality construction services that exceed client expectations. Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, adaptation to industry trends, and a commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in construction.
Our Philosophy

At QMAKS, we believe that successful construction projects are built on a foundation of collaboration, innovation, and integrity. Our philosophy centers on understanding our clients' needs and delivering solutions that align with their vision while adhering to the highest standards of quality and safety.

We approach each project with a commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and a focus on sustainable practices. Our goal is not just to construct buildings, but to create spaces that enhance lives, support communities, and stand the test of time.

The visionary behind our success

Eng. Qasim Makkawi

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

"Excellence in construction isn't just about building structures; it's about creating spaces that inspire, endure, and transform communities. At QMAKS, we don't just build for todayâ€”we build legacies for tomorrow."

With over 25 years of experience in the construction industry, Eng. Qasim Makkawi has established himself as a visionary leader and innovator. After graduating with honors in Civil Engineering from King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals, he spent a decade working with international construction firms before founding QMAKS in 2010.

Under his leadership, QMAKS has grown from a small local contractor to one of the region's most respected construction and development companies, with a portfolio of landmark projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Eng. Qasim is known for his unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and technological innovation. He has pioneered the adoption of Building Information Modeling (BIM) and green building practices in the region, earning numerous industry accolades for his forward-thinking approach.

Beyond his professional achievements, Eng. Qasim is deeply committed to community development and mentorship. He serves on the boards of several educational institutions and regularly speaks at industry conferences about the future of construction and sustainable development.
